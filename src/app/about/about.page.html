Pb Hub

Introducing PB Hub, an innovative e-commerce application that is designed to revolutionize online shopping by seamless and personalized experience for both users and administrators. PB Hub enables administrators to easily control their product listings, making it simple to add, remove, and modify products, including the option to take and showcase product photos directly within the application. PB Hub provides a wide range of functions for customers, such as adding or deleting items from their cart, searching and filtering products to locate specific ones, and personalizing their profiles with distinct profile pictures.
